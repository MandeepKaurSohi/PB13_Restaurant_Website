About PB13 Restaurant

Welcome to PB13, a culinary haven that brings the flavors of Punjab to your plate. Located in the heart of Sherpur, PB13 offers a delightful mix of tradition, taste, and hospitality. Whether you're craving authentic Punjabi dishes or exploring the richness of both vegetarian and non-vegetarian platters, PB13 is the perfect destination for food lovers.

Our menu boasts mouthwatering delicacies like buttery naans, flavorful curries, sizzling tandoori dishes, and a variety of rice-based specialties. Our vegetarian offerings include classics like paneer tikka, dal makhani, and seasonal sabzis, while non-veg lovers can indulge in tender butter chicken, juicy kebabs, and our signature tandoori fish.

At PB13, we pride ourselves on sourcing fresh ingredients and staying true to traditional recipes passed down through generations. With warm interiors and a friendly ambiance, we aim to provide an unforgettable dining experience that combines the best of Punjabi culture with modern comfort.
